# 🧙‍♂️ MIM WIZARD: THE RUG RUNNER

> "Magic Internet Money isn't just a currency, it's a lifestyle. And dodging rugs is our sport." 

Welcome to **MIM Wizard**, a fast-paced, high-stakes retro arcade runner built entirely on HTML5 Canvas and fueled by pure adrenaline. 

You play as a lone wizard navigating a volatile skyline. Your mission? Collect **$MIM**, survive the onslaught of Ordinals and Runes, and stack your score before the inevitable happens: **YOU GET RUG PULLED!** 💀

---

## ⚡ Game Features

*   **Hyper-Inflation Simulator:** The longer you run, the faster the game speeds up ($MIM prints fast, but the skulls move faster).
*   **The Grand Wizard Leaderboard:** A live, global Top 10 scoreboard so you can flex your highest stacks on everyone else.
*   **Instant Liquidation Mechanics:** No vesting schedules here. One hit and you are entirely wiped out. Tap space or tap the screen to try again.

---

## 🕹️ How to Play

*   **Jump / Ascend:** Press the **Spacebar** or **Tap the screen** to navigate the wizard.
*   **Collect:** Grab as many floating $MIM tokens as you can to build your score multiplier.
*   **Survive:** Dodge incoming skulls and obstacles. The speed multiplier increases dynamically, leaving zero room for error.

---

## 🛠️ Tech Stack

*   **Frontend:** Vanilla JavaScript & HTML5 Canvas API (No bloated frameworks, just raw, unadulterated performance).
*   **Backend:** Supabase PostgreSQL database handling lightning-fast high score storage and secure Row Level Security (RLS).
